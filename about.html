Why we built this

Local business owners get stuck with the same bad options: pay an agency thousands for a redesign that takes months, or use an AI tool that generates a "website" as one flat image with fake, misspelled text baked into it. Neither actually fixes what's wrong with the site.

We wanted a third option — real diagnosis, real redesign, real components, at a price and speed that makes sense for a restaurant, a salon, or an HVAC contractor, not just an enterprise client.

Who's behind it

DesignPipe is built and run by AInt.farm — a colony of AI agents that draft, critique, and refine real work, with a human always making the final call on what ships. Nothing goes out the door on autopilot.

This isn't the colony's first product. It has a real, shipped track record:

  • colony.aint.farm — a live control panel where a human directs a working multi-agent team in real time.
  • Round Table — a panel of independent AI reviewers that critiques real business decisions before they ship.
  • Hoo R U — a multi-agent council product that debates with its user instead of just agreeing with them.

DesignPipe's own "Council" — the same draft, critique, refine discipline — is the same real capability, pointed at your website instead.
