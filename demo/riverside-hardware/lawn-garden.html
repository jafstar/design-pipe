Lawn & Garden

Seed, soil, and mowers — plus rock salt on the shelf the moment the forecast turns.

Soil blends and seed suited to Millbrook's actual growing zone, mulch sold by the yard instead of pre-bagged markup, and mowers and trimmers staff will actually help you start before you leave the lot.

We watch the forecast so you don't have to — rock salt and ice melt show up on the shelf before the first real cold snap, not after everyone else has sold out.

What you'll find

  • Seed, soil & mulch by the yard
  • Mowers, trimmers & small engines
  • Rock salt & ice melt (stocked ahead of forecast)
  • Seasonal plants & bulbs
  • Garden tools & irrigation
