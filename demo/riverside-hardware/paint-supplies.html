Paint & Supplies

Custom color-matching done on-site while you wait — bring a chip, a photo, even a scrap of fabric.

Benjamin Moore is the backbone of the paint counter, matched on-site to whatever you bring in — a chip off the old trim, a photo on your phone, even a scrap of fabric if that's the only reference you've got. Most matches are ready before you've finished walking the rest of the store.

Brushes and rollers that don't shed, real prep supplies, and staff who'll walk you through sheen level and interior-vs-exterior formula before you buy the wrong can.

What you'll find

  • Benjamin Moore paint & primer
  • On-site custom color matching
  • Brushes, rollers & drop cloths
  • Stains, sealants & exterior finishes
  • Painter's tape & prep supplies

Have a question about paint & supplies?

Call or stop by — someone at the counter can help.
