Plumbing & Electrical

Fittings and fixtures big-box stores discontinued years ago. We still carry them.

Half the houses in Millbrook are older than the big-box stores' current catalog — so we kept ordering the fittings and fixtures they stopped carrying, because the town kept needing them. If a part looks obsolete, it's worth asking here before assuming you'll have to special-order it.

Real troubleshooting at the counter, not a self-checkout: describe the leak or the dead outlet, and you'll walk out with the right part the first time, not the third.

What you'll find

  • Pipe fittings & fixtures (including discontinued sizes)
  • Wiring, outlets & breakers
  • Water heaters & shutoff valves
  • Plumbing & electrical tool rental
  • Free at-counter troubleshooting

Have a question about plumbing & electrical?

Call or stop by — someone at the counter can help.
