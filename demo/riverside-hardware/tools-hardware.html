Tools & Hardware

Hand tools, fasteners, and power tools from Milwaukee & DeWalt — organized the way people actually think about a project, not by SKU.

This is the aisle most people come in for, and it's the one we've spent the most years getting right. Hand tools, fasteners, and power tools from Milwaukee and DeWalt sit exactly where a working person expects them — grouped by job, not by manufacturer catalog. Need a bit for a specific screw and don't know its name? Bring the screw. Someone at the counter will match it from the bulk bins in under a minute.

We also run a quiet battery-and-blade counter most stores skip entirely: replacement batteries, blades, and bits for the cordless tools half of Millbrook already owns, plus basic tool repair for the stuff that's worth fixing instead of replacing.

What you'll find

  • Hand tools & fasteners
  • Milwaukee & DeWalt power tools
  • Drill bits, blades & battery packs
  • Basic tool repair & battery exchange
  • Bulk hardware bins (nuts, bolts, screws)

Not sure which tool you need?

Bring in the broken part or a photo — we'll help you find the right one.
